# Getting Started with Eluru Connect 🚀

**Who is this for?** Anyone aged 15+ who wants to book a public venue in Eluru, AP — parks, stadiums, community halls, and more.

---

## What is Eluru Connect?

Eluru Connect is an **online portal** run by the Eluru Municipal Corporation. It lets citizens book public venues fairly — no phone calls, no bribes, no backdoor deals. Everything is transparent and anyone can see the full list of bookings.

---

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

1. Visit the app (or open on your browser)
2. Click **"Create an account"**
3. Enter:
   - Your **Full Name** (as per Aadhaar)
   - Your **10-digit phone number**
   - A **password** (at least 6 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You're registered! Now sign in.

> 💡 **Tip:** Your phone number is your login ID — remember it!

---

## Step 2 — Login

1. Enter your **phone number** and **password**
2. Click **"Sign In"**
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard**

---

## Step 3 — Book Your First Venue

1. Click **"Browse Venues"** in the left sidebar
2. Scroll through the list of venues in Eluru
3. Click **"Book Slot"** on the venue you want
4. In the popup:
   - Select a **Start Date & Time**
   - Select an **End Date & Time**
5. Click **"Confirm Booking"**
6. Done! 🎉 You'll see it in your Dashboard.

---

## Step 4 — Check In On the Day

- When your booking time approaches, go to **Dashboard**
- Click the **"Check-in"** button (available 30 minutes before your slot)
- This confirms you actually used the venue

---

## Accounts & Roles

| Role | Who | Perks |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Citizen** | Regular public | 4 bookings/month per venue |
| **VIP** | Official organisations (MLA offices, etc.) | 8 bookings/month + prime-time priority |
| **Venue Manager** | Municipal staff | Can create/manage venues |
| **Govt Super Admin** | District Collector office | Full system control + audit access |
