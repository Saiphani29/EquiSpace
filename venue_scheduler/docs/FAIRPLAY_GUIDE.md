# Fair-Play System — Complete Guide ⚖️

---

## Why Does Fair-Play Exist?

Public venues belong to everyone. The Fair-Play system ensures people **use what they book** and don't waste shared community resources. It's the same idea as a sports referee — everyone plays by the same rules.

---

## How Strikes Work

You receive a **Fair-Play Strike** when:

| Action | Penalty |
|--------|---------|
| Missing check-in (No-Show) | +1 Strike |
| (Future) Reported dirty venue | +1 Strike |

### Strike Limits

| Strikes | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| 0 | Perfect score — 100% Fair-Play |
| 1 | Warning — 66% score |
| 2 | Second warning — 33% score |
| 3 | **Account Suspended** — cannot book anything |

---

## Check-In and No-Shows

The check-in window is **30 minutes before** to **30 minutes after** your booking start time.

If you don't check in within this window:
1. Your booking is automatically marked as `no_show`
2. You receive **1 fair-play strike**
3. The slot is released to the **waitlist** so someone else gets it
4. If this is your 3rd strike, your account is **immediately suspended**

---

## Getting Unsuspended

If your account is suspended:
1. Contact the **Eluru Municipal Office**
2. A `GOVT_SUPER_ADMIN` can clear your strikes and reinstate your account
3. You'll receive confirmation once reinstated

## Quota System

Citizens are limited to **4 bookings per venue per month** to ensure fair access. This prevents one person from monopolising popular venues.

| Role | Monthly Quota Per Venue |
|------|------------------------|
| Citizen | 4 bookings |
| VIP | 8 bookings |
| Govt Admin | Unlimited |

---

## VIP Prime-Time Reservation

**Prime-time slots** = Sunday mornings (before 12 PM noon).

- These slots are **reserved for VIP/Government** up until 48 hours before the event
- After that 48-hour mark, they **open to all citizens** automatically
- This allows official organisations to plan ahead without permanently blocking citizens

### Example
> An event is scheduled for Sunday 10 AM. It's currently Wednesday.
> - Wednesday → Friday: Only VIP/Govt can book
> - Friday 10 AM onwards (48 hours before): Open to everyone

## Your Fair-Play Score

The Fair-Play Score is visible on:
- **Dashboard** — top-right badge
- **Settings** — detailed breakdown with strike bars

Formula: `100% - (strikes × 34%)`

| Strikes | Score |
|---------|-------|
| 0 | 100% 🟢 |
| 1 | 66% 🟡 |
| 2 | 33% 🟠 |
| 3 | 0% — Suspended 🔴 |

## Tips to Keep a Good Score

- ✅ Always cancel bookings you won't use (frees the slot for others)
- ✅ Set a phone reminder before your booking slot
- ✅ Check in as soon as the window opens (30 min before)
- ❌ Don't book "just in case" — only book when you're sure
- ❌ Don't leave venues messy (peer-review feature coming soon)
